About

Building-level knowledge.
Personal accountability.

Nicole James began her real estate career in Austin in 2007. Over the next decade her practice consolidated around downtown Austin condominium living — and the results showed up inside specific buildings.

Portrait

A nineteen-year specialty practice in downtown Austin condominium towers.

More than 90 units sold at Bridges on The Park alone, with the highest sales prices repeatedly over the last ten years and among the shortest days on market in the building. Christie's International Real Estate recognized her as a Luxury Specialist in 2020. A growing share of her transactions close privately, before hitting the public market.

Nicole was nominated for the Austin Business Journal's Top 50 Residential Real Estate Agents in 2011, 2012, and 2014. Representation runs through @properties Lone Star, the Christie's International Real Estate affiliate serving Austin from 1105 N. Lamar.
