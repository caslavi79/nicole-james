Nicole represents buyers and sellers across downtown Austin's condominium towers. Her focus is the urban core: high-rise and mid-rise condos in and around downtown, the Rainey Street district, Market District, Second Street District, Seaholm, and the Warehouse District.

Nicole has been licensed in Texas since 2007. She began her practice in Austin, narrowed to downtown condos within her first several years, and has been nominated for the Austin Business Journal's Top 50 Residential Real Estate Agents in 2011, 2012, and 2014.
